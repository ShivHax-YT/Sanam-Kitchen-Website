A winter staple that feels like a hug. The chili is optional but adds a subtle warmth.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups milk
- 100g dark chocolate, chopped
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 2 tbsp sugar or maple syrup
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of chili flakes
- Pinch of sea salt
- Whipped cream to top (optional)

## Steps
1. Warm milk in a saucepan until steaming.
2. Whisk in cocoa, sugar, cinnamon, and chili until smooth.
3. Add chopped chocolate; stir until melted and silky.
4. Pour into mugs, finish with sea salt and whipped cream.

## Tips
- Use half milk, half cream for an ultra-rich version.
- Replace sugar with jaggery for caramel notes.
