Breakfast meal-prep that tastes like a caf√© treat. The chai spice mix warms the oats while jaggery and dates add caramel sweetness.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup milk (or oat milk)
- 1/2 cup yogurt
- 1 tbsp jaggery or brown sugar
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1/8 tsp ginger powder
- Pinch of clove powder
- 2 dates, chopped
- Toasted nuts and berries to top

## Steps
1. Combine oats, milk, yogurt, jaggery, chia seeds, and spices in a jar.
2. Stir in chopped dates. Seal and refrigerate overnight.
3. Next morning, loosen with a splash of milk, top with nuts and berries.

## Tips
- For extra protein, stir in a scoop of Greek yogurt or protein powder.
- Warm it briefly in the microwave for a cozy version.
