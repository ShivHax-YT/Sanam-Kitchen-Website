A lighter, plant-forward main that still feels indulgent. Serve with mint chutney and lemon wedges.

## Ingredients
- 1 large cauliflower, cut into 3 thick steaks
- 1/2 cup yogurt
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp mustard oil or neutral oil
- Salt to taste

## Steps
1. Whisk yogurt, spices, lemon, oil, and salt into a thick marinade.
2. Coat cauliflower steaks generously; marinate 20 minutes.
3. Roast at 220°C (425°F) for 25-30 minutes, broiling the last 2 minutes for char.
4. Serve with chutney, pickled onions, and naan.

## Tips
- Add a spoon of smoked paprika for extra smokiness.
- Air-fry at 200°C (390°F) for 18-20 minutes for faster cooking.
