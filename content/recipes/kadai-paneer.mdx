Smoky, spicy, and quick—this kadai paneer leans on freshly ground coriander and Kashmiri chili for deep color without overpowering heat.

## Ingredients
- 300g paneer, cubed
- 1 cup onions, diced
- 1 cup bell peppers, diced
- 3 tomatoes, pureed
- 2 tbsp kadai masala (roast coriander, cumin, fennel, red chili, blend)
- 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp kasuri methi
- 2 tbsp cream
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste

## Steps
1. Sauté onions and peppers on high heat for light char; set aside.
2. In the same pan, add oil and ginger-garlic; cook until aromatic.
3. Pour tomato puree, kadai masala, and salt; cook until oil separates.
4. Add paneer, sautéed veggies, kasuri methi, and cream. Simmer 3 minutes.
5. Finish with fresh coriander and squeeze of lime.

## Tips
- Toast the spice mix fresh for the signature kadai aroma.
- Add a few slit green chilies if you like extra heat.
