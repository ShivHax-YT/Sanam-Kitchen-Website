A cozy bowl of pasta that leans on pantry staples and fresh basil. The sauce is balanced with a little chili heat and a swirl of cream for richness.

## Ingredients
- 250g penne or rigatoni
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 can crushed tomatoes (400g)
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 cup cream
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan
- 1/2 cup fresh basil, torn
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Steps
1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until translucent.
3. Add tomato paste and chili flakes; cook until the paste darkens slightly.
4. Pour in crushed tomatoes, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer 10 minutes.
5. Stir in cream and parmesan. Add a splash of pasta water if sauce is too thick.
6. Toss pasta with sauce and basil. Adjust seasoning and serve hot.

## Tips
- Finish with extra basil oil or chili crisp for a premium touch.
- Swap cream with cashew cream to keep it dairy-light.
