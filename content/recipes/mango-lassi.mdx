## Ingredients
- 2 ripe mangoes or 1 cup pulp
- 1 cup thick yogurt
- 1/2 cup cold milk
- 2 tbsp honey or sugar
- Pinch of saffron soaked in warm milk
- 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
- Ice cubes as needed

## Steps
1. Blend mango, yogurt, milk, honey, saffron milk, and cardamom until silky.
2. Adjust sweetness; add ice and blend briefly for a frothy top.
3. Serve chilled with pistachio slivers.

## Tips
- Swap milk with coconut milk for a tropical twist.
- Freeze leftover lassi into popsicles.
