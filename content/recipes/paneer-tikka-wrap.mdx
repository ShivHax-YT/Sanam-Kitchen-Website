A street-food style wrap that doubles as an impressive party snack. Yogurt-marinated paneer stays juicy while the chutney slaw keeps everything fresh.

## Ingredients
- 300g paneer, cut into strips
- 1/2 cup yogurt
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt to taste
- 1 cup sliced onions and peppers
- 4 rotis or tortillas
- 1/2 cup green chutney
- 1/4 cup mayo or yogurt
- Pickled onions to serve

## Steps
1. Marinate paneer with yogurt, spices, lemon, and salt for 20 minutes.
2. Sear paneer and veggies on high heat until lightly charred.
3. Mix chutney with mayo to make a slaw dressing; toss with sliced onions.
4. Warm rotis, spread chutney slaw, add paneer and veggies, roll tight.
5. Toast on a dry pan for 1 minute per side for a crisp finish.

## Tips
- Add fries or lettuce for extra crunch.
- Swap paneer with tofu for a lighter wrap.
