The fastest way to upgrade breakfast. Fluffy eggs meet melty cheese and a green chutney swipe for brightness.

## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 tbsp chopped onions
- 2 tbsp chopped peppers
- 1 green chili, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
- 2 slices cheese
- 4 slices sourdough or milk bread
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp green chutney
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Steps
1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper. Stir in onions, peppers, chili, and cilantro.
2. Cook omelette in butter until just set; place cheese on top to melt.
3. Toast bread with butter; spread chutney on one side.
4. Sandwich omelette between bread slices. Press lightly and slice.

## Tips
- Add a sprinkle of chaat masala for street-style flavor.
- Swap bread with paratha for a heartier roll.
